Our Story

This resort is the brain child of two partners Dr.Kee Swee Ann & Tribhuvan Singh. Dr. Kee is from Malaysia ,a highly decorative Nephrologists , besides he is also in nature conservation & developing tourist stay with the idea to preserve the nature of the area. He is also running a resort in Tioman Island in Malaysia since last 10years with same essence of tourism. Tribhuvan is local from Jodhpur having a vast experience in Human Resource Management & Tourism Industry .He has also worked for sustainable development projects & experiential tourism in different parts of Rajasthan.

Both of them met in Jodhpur as a Guest & Tourist Guide in Jodhpur. Both having a common interest, of nature conservation & promoting rural, experiential & sustainable development tourism; ventured together to develop Impain Inn. We are being supported & helped by many friends coming from different industries across the world.


About Us

We are situated in the heart of village Manaklao. This is around 22 kms from the city Jodhpur. Jodhpur is 2nd largest city of Rajasthan also know as cultural capital of Rajasthan. This city is also known as Blue City. Manaklao is a blend of different communities’ inhabitation there. According to the folk lures village is named after a Pond of Water. There were brother and sister named “Manak” & “Manai” respectively, and the only pond of the area was named after brother i.e. Manak, If the name is split according to local dialect, it means Manak-Talao (Pond of Manak). Major occupation here is Agriculture, pottery making, ironsmith, Indian day bed making &embroidery. Some of the occupations are now done by modern techniques, while the others are still being practiced in traditional ways. Village earned its fame by the social work of Padamshri Dr. Narayan Singh Manaklao. Especially for the drug addict rehabilitation center and Sucheta Kriplani Shiksha Niketan for specially abled & handicap children.

Impian Inn, notably located at the Mathaniyaa Highway, in the countryside of Jodhpur, is the mirage that you have always been looking for! A stay that emanates serenity, luxury and authenticity with lush green surroundings of garden and a swimming pool. Impian Resort gives you a fresh, pristine and rejuvenated feeling. The hotel offers 10 well-appointed chalets and 4 luxurious tent and urbane furnished rooms. The nature inspired decor of the hotel, and the luxuriously furnished rooms, overlook the mathaniyaa Highway. The rooms have all modern amenities of a modern Hotel .
